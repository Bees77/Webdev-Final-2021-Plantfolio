Plant Uses

Nutrition

AAAA I love agriculture, especially permaculture. Both are designed around edible plant production, but if done properly, create sustainable and lasting food sources for more species than just humans.

Air cleansing

This isn't really a use for plants, but more a property. Through photosynthesis, all plants produce a significant portion of the oxygen in our atmosphere. Usually trees are more heavy producers than herbs and woody shrubs, but all plants posess this quality, making them invaluable.

Fibers

All organic mater contains fiber, but plant fibers can be especcially tough, especially palms and yuccas, giving them a wide variety of human uses.

Dyes

Most natural dyes come from plants like indigo and mustard, which create strong dyes for clothing and art. You can actually grow your own dye garden, here's a reference as to how- Dye Gardens
